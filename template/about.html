Who We Are

Ikageng Day Care Centre, located in Hammanskraal, is dedicated to providing quality care and education to young children in the rural community. Our goal is to create a safe and nurturing environment where children can thrive emotionally, socially, and academically.

Our Mission

Our mission at Ikageng Day Care Centre is to foster the holistic development of children through love, education, and play. We are committed to empowering the children of Hammanskraal with the skills they need for a brighter future.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and excellence in early childhood education, contributing to the growth and development of the next generation of leaders in South Africa.
