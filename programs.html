Explore Our Programs

At Ikageng Day Care Centre, we offer a variety of programs designed to nurture and develop the skills and creativity of every child. Our programs cater to different age groups and focus on creating a fun and educational experience for all children.

Early Learning Program (Ages 2-4)

Our Early Learning Program focuses on introducing children to basic concepts like numbers, colors, and shapes. Through play-based learning, we help children develop social skills and emotional confidence.

Pre-School Program (Ages 4-6)

Designed for children preparing for primary school, this program builds foundational skills in reading, writing, and mathematics. We provide a structured learning environment that encourages curiosity and exploration.

Arts and Crafts

This program stimulates children's creativity and imagination by engaging them in fun arts and crafts projects. From drawing and painting to creating unique crafts, children get to express themselves and learn new skills.

Outdoor Play and Physical Activities

We believe in the importance of physical play for the healthy development of children. Our outdoor activities include sports, games, and free play in a safe and supervised environment.

Music and Movement

Children love to move! Our music and movement program integrates dance, music, and rhythmic activities to help children improve their coordination and motor skills while having fun.

Storytelling and Language Development

In this program, children engage in listening to stories and participating in activities that encourage language development. We foster a love for reading and storytelling, which helps improve vocabulary and comprehension.
